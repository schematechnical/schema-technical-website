Service Network Architecture (SNA)

Operationalise your field support. We build the objective, turnkey architecture required to safely hand over hardware maintenance to third-party depots.

The Ugly Truth

Handing medical hardware to a distributor without strict vendor qualification and objective training rubrics is a massive regulatory liability. Without an architecture, you lose control of your physical product quality in the field.

Exact Deliverables & Methodology

We operate as your outsourced engineering agent to establish a legally defensible third-party repair network. This engagement is strictly bounded to operational rollout.

Phase 0: Technical Discovery

Rapid triage of the physical device to validate mechanical complexity (waived if a Service Design Review has previously been executed).

Phase 1: Vendor Qualification (ISO 13485 Cl 7.4)

Remote screening of prospective depots, followed by a formal physical site audit to validate facility ESD controls, calibration limits, IT security, and inventory segregation.

Phase 2: Curriculum Engineering

Translation of your released Service Manual into a structured Master Training Matrix, engineering binary Pass/Fail grading rubrics for all practical assessments.

Phase 3: Implementation & Certification (ISO 13485 Cl 7.5.3)

Deployment of a Lead Systems Engineer for up to 4 days on-site at the target depot. Delivery of the "Train the Trainer" cohort, culminating in signed Training Records (ST-REC-TRN-001).

Who This Service is NOT For

Clients missing base documentation. SNA is strictly an operational rollout package. It does not include SOP or manual writing. You must provide formally released, QARA-approved Service Manuals prior to Phase 2. We will not build training matrices based on verbal R&D intent.

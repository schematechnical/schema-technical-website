Bridge the Gap Between Lab & Field.

Founded by Dan Howes (ex-Bio-Rad, Thermo Fisher, RAF), Schema Technical brings the rigour of aerospace logistics to MedTech.

Having led the support architecture for complex systems like the Bio-Rad Genesis, we know exactly what happens when R&D prototypes hit the real world. A brilliant clinical device will fail commercially if the service infrastructure isn't built to match the engineering quality.

Embedded within the Cambridge, Norwich, and London MedTech hubs, we provide fractional service architecture to scaling device manufacturers nationwide and internationally.

We are not generalist management consultants. We are Service Architects. We take the 'Tribal Knowledge' from your R&D team and convert it into audit-ready SOPs and validated service protocols (Clause 7.5). We ensure your 'Servicing' folder is as robust as your 'Design' folder. This allows your engineers to focus on the next product, while we ensure the current one stays reliable in the field.

Serviceability Engineering as a Service

A modular framework to protect your R&D team and scale your support.

PRE-LAUNCH | ISO 13485:7.5.1

Service Design Review (SDR)

Ensure your "Design Transfer" includes Serviceability. We perform forensic teardowns to identify maintenance risks and validate repair access before you freeze the design.

What We Deliver:

  • Risk Assessment: Component-level failure risk ranking.
  • Accessibility Analysis: Time-to-repair optimisation.
  • The "Red Flag" Report: Critical changes needed before tooling freeze.
ROLLOUT | ISO 13485:7.5.3

Service Network Architecture

Scale without headcount. We build the "Turnkey" support package that allows you to hand over support to distributors or 3rd parties.

What We Deliver:

  • SOP Engineering: Converting "Tribal Knowledge" into field manuals.
  • Logistics Index: Defining Van Stock vs. Depot Stock.
  • "Train the Trainer": Certifying your first external partners.
SUSTAINING | ISO 13485:8.2.1

Field Root-Cause Analysis (FRA)

Turn "No Fault Found" returns into compliant Post-Market Surveillance evidence. We perform forensic Field Root-Cause Analysis (FRA) to protect your margins and provide the exact closed-loop data required to update your ISO 14971 Risk Management files.

What We Deliver:

  • Field Root-Cause Analysis: Deep-dive into early failed units.
  • ISO 14971 Data Loop: Translating field failure data into structured engineering feedback for R&D and QA risk management updates.
  • Spare Parts Forecast: Usage-based inventory optimisation.
DOCUMENTATION | ISO 13485:7.5

Service Documentation

We fill the compliance gap. We convert R&D data into audit-ready Technical Service Manuals, IQ/OQ protocols, and detailed Maintenance Checklists.

What We Deliver:

  • Technical Service Manual (TSM): The definitive guide for repair.
  • Install Guides & Site Readiness: Protocols for first-time safe deployments.
  • IQ/OQ Protocols: Installation and Operational Qualification validation.
  • PPM Schedules & Checklists: Preventative maintenance for field engineering.
  • Service Software Validation (SSV): Proof of reliability for diagnostic tools.
